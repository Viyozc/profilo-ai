    Why freelance profiles are a power-law game

    The skills are rarely the problem. The profile is.

    Invites beat proposals

    Top freelancers get invited to jobs they never applied for. Invited work converts better and costs zero connects. A strong profile is the lever that flips you from "applier" to "invited".

    Outcome beats tools

    "I build SaaS MVPs that ship in 2 weeks" outsells "React Developer" every time. A title with the buyer's outcome is the single highest-leverage fix on your profile.

    Proof kills doubt

    Profiles with real results and case studies win the invite. Specificity (one named win with numbers) beats a wall of vague past projects.

    Specialists out-earn generalists

    Clients pay more for a named result ("Shopify migration") than a generalist ("web dev"). One sharp niche beats a broad menu that signals "I'll take anything".

    FAQ

    Is the audit really free?

    Yes. The on-page audit runs entirely in your browser and costs nothing. No account needed.

    Do I need to connect my freelance account?

    No. You describe your setup with a few clicks and paste your title. Profilo scores the strategy, not your private client data — so it works before you've optimized anything.

    How is the score calculated?

    We weight title & keywords, positioning / overview, portfolio proof, skills framing, niche focus, and profile completeness & responsiveness — the signals that decide whether a stranger invites you or scrolls past.

    Will this guarantee I get hired?

    No tool can. But most of the gap between a profile that sits and one that gets invited is operational — title, overview, proof, niche. Profilo shows you exactly where your gaps are.
